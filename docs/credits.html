Asset Credits

This page tracks what assets are allowed in the public app and what must stay as reference only.

Village Home Base

Use rules

Assets can be used directly only when they are original, commissioned with permission, owned by you, CC0, CC-BY, MIT, or otherwise clearly licensed for reuse.

Stardew Valley, Fields of Mistria, mod assets, Pinterest images, and other game assets should stay as inspiration unless the creator or licence clearly allows reuse outside that game.

Built-in starter pack

Starter Cottage Pack contains original Esslay placeholder furniture made for this app: writing desk, geode shelf, and potted ivy.

These are safe built-in assets and do not require external credit.

Imported local assets

Imported assets are saved in your browser. They are useful for testing the look of the game, but they should not be committed into the public repo unless their licence is known and safe.
